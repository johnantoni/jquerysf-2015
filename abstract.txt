3. What can you do with an AST? A whole lot. Most of the things you can do are
terrible hacks but sometimes there is no other way. This talk will cover some
applications of ASTs to solve problems when there is no other way.

Modules covered:

* browserify
* falafel
* coverify
* insert-module-globals
* static-module
* brfs
* glslify
* static-eval
* live-patch
